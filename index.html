The Practice

Beauty is a lens, not a limit. Starting with professional makeup artistry — editorial, bridal, SFX — the work expanded naturally into directing the image itself: shaping the visual concept, the casting, the mood, the story behind the frame.

From there, an MA in Creative Direction at Solent University deepened the discipline. Now that same visual thinking is applied to social media: building brand identities and content strategies that are as intentional and considered as any photographic shoot.

The Gender Stage

Editorial Series

This editorial series was inspired by meeting people who would identify as someone other than male or female and understanding by time how gender is something more performative than being a fix set of rules. As humans, we learn through every stage of our lives and as the mind grows we tend to shape into our best selves, digging deeper and deeper within. Seeing gender as a stage and us as being performers, being who we truly want is the main theme of this shoot.

Model — Kolby
Photographer — Georgie Birdsall
Direction — Ayushi Burman
MUA — Laura Caitlin

He / She / Or
They / Them?

A project on what they call a "Third Gender"

While Western civilizations have only lately explored genders other than male and female, the Hijra community in India has been known in history for almost 4,000 years and is described in ancient literature as a testament to the importance of sexual variety in Indian culture, which is widely ignored nowadays.

People who identify as transgender are still fighting for their rights in our society as a result of society's refusal to recognize their gender identities. As a result, prejudice, social injustice, and physical assault continue to plague them.

Model — Tamoghno
Photographer — Bishnu Goenka
Direction — Ayushi Burman & Nuaman Kabir
MUA — Ayushi Burman

Confusion

A Mind's Misery

No one talks about it proudly. The anxiety and the fear in one's mind to decide absolutely anything! Not everyone has been given the power to think about both sides of a coin for every single situation. But should we even call this a power?

When we can see both perspectives clearly like two different colors, say it Black or White and you find pros and cons of both — which one would you end up choosing?

Let Me Be!

Self-love & Empowerment

Being judgemental is one of the worst things humans can do. It not only brings down someone's confidence but can change the way how someone chooses to live their entire life. This project focuses on finding true love and empowerment within oneself and against all the voices in the head that tells you that you are not enough!
